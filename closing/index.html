Help Rebuild Crown Cafe's Future

Our journey started in August 2021, with a dream of creating a place where the community could gather and share moments over delicious meals. Crown Cafe became that place, nestled in the heart of Watertown, MA. Now, as we face the challenging times of having to close our doors on March 24, 2024, due to circumstances beyond our control, we reach out to you, our community, for support.

Every contribution to our GoFundMe campaign helps us take a step towards a new beginning. Donate here and learn more about our story and how your support can make a difference.
